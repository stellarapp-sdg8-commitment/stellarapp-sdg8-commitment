## Our contribution to SDG 8

StellarApp supports SDG 8 in several ways:

- promoting **fair and transparent employment** through verified reputation  
- making it easier for people to **find real jobs** in Spain and Europe  
- helping employers build a culture of **recognition and merit**  
- giving visibility to **employees with good performance**, even in sectors where CVs are not enough  

We focus especially on:

- hospitality  
- retail  
- services with direct contact with customers  
- other sectors where personal reputation is key
